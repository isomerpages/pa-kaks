<p>The ValuePlus programme supports residents by letting them buy two essential
items for just $1. Through polling, residents get to decide the items they
want most â€” making every edition resident-led and relevant.</p>
<p>Now into its fifth edition, ValuePlus benefits more than 3,000 residents
monthly. It has also become more than just a good deal. Reflecting a strong
kampung spirit, neighbours help each other and those with mobility issues
collect the items. By easing daily expenses and involving the community
in the process, the programme strengthens bonds and uplifts households
through practical help.</p>
